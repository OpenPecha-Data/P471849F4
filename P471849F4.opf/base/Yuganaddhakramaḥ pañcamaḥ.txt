[5]yuganaddhakramaḥ pañcamaḥnamaḥ śrīvajradharāyaphalahetvātmakaṃ nāthaṃ sarvadvandvavivarjitam|praṇamya
likhyate samyag yuganaddhakramottamaḥ

saṃsāro nirvṛtiśceti kalpanādvayavarjanāt|ekībhāvo bhaved yatra yuganaddhaṃ taducyate

saṅkleśaṃ vyavadānaṃ ca jñātvā tu paramārthataḥ|ekībhāvaṃ tu yo vettiṃ sa vetti yuganaddhakam

sākārabhāvasaṅkalpaṃ nirākāratvakalpanām|ekīkṛtya cerad yogī sa vetti yuganaddhakam

grāhyaṃ ca grāhakaṃ caiva dvidhā buddhirna vidyate|abhinnatā bhaved yatra tadāha yuganaddhakam

śāśvatocchedabuddhī tu yaḥ prahāya pravartate|yuganaddhakramākhyaṃ vai tattvaṃ vetti sa paṇḍitaḥ

prajñākaruṇayoraikyaṃ jñātvā yatra pravartate|yuganaddha iti khyātaḥ kramo'yaṃ buddhagocaraḥ

prajñopāyasamāpattyā jñātvā sarvaṃ samāsataḥ|yatra sthito mahāyogī tad bhaved yuganaddhakam

yatra sopadhiśeṣaṃ ca tathā'nupadhiśeṣakam|ityevaṃ kalpanā nāsti tat taddhi yuganaddhakam

yatra pudgalanairātmyaṃ dharmanairātmyamityapi|kalpanāyā viviktatvaṃ yuganaddhasya lakṣaṇam

jñātvā krameṇa tattvajñaḥ svādhiṣṭhānaprabhāsvaram|tayoreva samājaṃ yad yuganaddhakramo hyayam

piṇḍagrāhanubhedābhyāṃ praveśastathatālaye|utthānaṃ ca tato yatra samantād yuganaddhakam

saṃvṛtiṃ paramārthaṃ ca pṛthag jñātvā vibhāgataḥ|sammīlanaṃ bhaved yatra yuganaddhaṃ taducyate

tathātalambanaṃ naiva vyutthānaṃ yatra naiva hi|yuganaddhaṃ bhavet tacca yogināṃ padamavyayam

suptaḥ prabuddha ityetadavasthādvayavarjitam|yuganaddhaṃ vadecchāstā svāpabodhavivarjitam

samādhānāsamādhānaṃ yasya nāstyeva sarvathā|yuganaddhe sthito yogī bhāvābhāvavivarjitaḥ

asmṛtismṛtinirmuktaḥ satatodayalakṣaṇaḥ|vicaredicchayā yogī yuganaddhakrame sthitaḥ

rāgārāgavinirmuktaḥ paramānandamūrtimān|āsaṃsāraṃ sthitiṃ kuryād yuganaddhavibhāvakaḥ

kāryaṃ ca kāraṇaṃ caiva kṛtvā'bhinnaṃsvabhāvataḥ|yā sthitiryogināṃ buddhā yuganaddhaṃ vadanti
tat

utpattikrama eko'yam utpannakrama ityapi|ekatvaṃ tu dvayoryatra yuganaddhastaducyate

devatā pariśuddheyam aśuddheyaṃ bhavediti|iti yā kalpanā'bhinnā yatra tad yuganaddhakam

rūpīti cāpyarūpīti kalpanādvayavarjanāt|yaḥśāntiṃ vetti yogīndraḥ sa prāpto yuganaddhakam

evaṃ vai yaḥ sthito yogī yuganaddhakrame sthitaḥ|ucyate sa hi sarvajñaḥ tattvadarśī ca viśvadhṛk

māyājālābhisambuddhaḥ saṃsārārṇavapāragaḥ|kṛtakṛtyo mahāyogī satyadvayanaye sthitaḥ

etadevādvayaṃ jñānam apratiṣṭhitanirvṛtiḥ|buddhatvaṃ vajrasattvatvaṃ sarvaiśvaryaṃ tathaiva
ca||25|vajropamasamādhistu niṣpannakrama eva ca|vajropamasamādhiścāpyadvayaṃ tacca kathyate

anutpādādayaḥ śabdā advayajñānasūcakāḥ|asyaiva vācakāḥ sarve nānyat tatrābhidhīyate

mahāmudrātmikāṃ siddhiṃ sadasatpakṣavarjitām|anenaiva gatā buddhā gaṅgāyāḥ sikatopamāḥ

ghaṭamāno mahāyogī yuganaddhapade sthitaḥ|bhāvayed yuganaddhaṃ tu caryāṃ cāpi tadanvayām

yathātmani tathā śatrau yathā bhāryā tathātmajā|yathā mātā tathā vaiśyā yathā ḍombī tathā dvijā
