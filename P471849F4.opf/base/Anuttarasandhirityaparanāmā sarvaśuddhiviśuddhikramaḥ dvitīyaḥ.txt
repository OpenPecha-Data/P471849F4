[2]anuttarasandhirityaparanāmāsarvaśuddhiviśuddhikramaḥ dvitīyaḥnamaḥ śrīvajrasattvāyanamaste'stu
namaste'stu namaste'stu namo namaḥ|evaṃ stute namaste'stu kaḥ stotā kaśca saṃstutaḥ

yathā jalaṃ jale nyastaṃ ghṛtaṃ caiva yathā ghṛte|svakīyaṃ ca svayaṃ paśyej jñānaṃ yatreha vandanā

kintu sarvajñagatibhirvinā tannopalabhyate|tamaḥpaṭalasañchannaṃ prasādād dvīpamāpnuyāt

śūnyaṃ ca atiśūnyaṃ ca mahāśunyaṃ tṛtīyakam|caturthaṃ sarvaśūnyaṃ ca phalahetuprabhedataḥ

prajñopāyasamāyogānniṣpannamupalabdhakam|upalabdhācca niṣpannāt sarvaśūnyaṃ prabhāsvaram

hetukramaviśuddhaṃ tu vijñānatrayayogataḥ|śūnyatrayasamāyogāllabhyate'nuttaraṃ padam

ālokaśśūnyaṃ prajñā ca cittaṃ ca paratantrakam|tasyedānīṃ pravakṣyāmi prakṛtispharaṇaṃ sphuṭam

virāgo madhyamaścaiva adhimātrastathaiva ca|manogatāgataṃ caiva śokāditṛtīyaṃ tathā

saumyaṃ vikampo bhītaśca madhyabhīto'tibhītakaḥ|tṛṣṇā madhyatṛṣṇā cātitṛosṇoopādānakaṃ tathā

niḥśubhaṃ kṣuttṛṣā caiva vedanā samavedanā|ativedanā kṣaṇaścaiva vettividdhāraṇāpadam

pratyavekṣaṇaṃ lajjā ca kāruṇyaṃ snehatastrayam|cakitaṃ saṃśayaścaiva mātsaryaṃ ceti kīrtitāḥ

trayastriṃśat prakṛtayaḥ svasaṃvedyāḥ śarīriṇām|saṃvṛtisphuṭarupeṇa niśāsaṃjñā pradarśitāḥ

strīsaṃjñā tathā proktā mandākārā tathaiva ca|vāmasaṃjñā punaścaiva candramaṇḍalapaṅkajam

dṛḍhīkaraṇahetutvāt sabinduḥ prathamaḥ svaraḥ|niśākarāṃśusaṅkāśa ālokajñānasambhavaḥ

ālokābhāsamityutkam atiśūnyamupāyakam|parikalpitaṃ tathā proktaṃ proktaṃ caitasikaṃ tathā

rāgo raktaṃ tathā tuṣṭaṃ madhyatuṣṭātituṣṭakam|harṣaṇaṃ caiva prāmodyaṃ vismayo hasitaṃ tathā

hlādanāliṅganaṃ caiva tathā cumbanacūṣaṇam|dhairyaṃ vīryaṃ ca mānaśca kartṛhartṛbalāni ca

utsāhaḥ sāhasaṃ caiva tathā cottamasāhasam|madhyamaṃ sāhasaṃ rādraṃ vilāso vairameva ca

śubhaṃ ca vāk sphuṭā satyamasatyaṃ niścayastathā|nirupādānadātṛtve codanaṃ śūratā tathā|alajjā
dhūrtaduṣṭaśca haṭhaḥ kuṭila eva ca|catvāriṃśat prakṛtayaḥ kṣaṇikāścātiśūnyajjāḥ

divāpuruṣasaṃjñā ca kharākāraśca dakṣiṇaḥ|sūryamaṇḍalasaṃjñā ca vajrasaṃjñā tathaiva ca

kalā saiva tu vijñeyā bindudvayavibhūṣitā|divākarāṃśuisaṅkāśā ālokābhāsayogajā

ālokasyopalabdhiśca upalabdhaṃ tathaiva ca|pariniṣpannakaṃ caiva avidyā caiva nāmataḥ

mahāśūnyapadasyaite paryāyāḥ kathitā jinaiḥ|madhyarāgakṣaṇaścaiva vismṛtirbhrāntireva ca

tūṣṇīṃbhāvaśca khedaśca ālasyaṃ dandhatā tathā|avidyāyāḥ kṣaṇāḥ sapta vijñeyāḥ sukṣmayogibhiḥ

na bījaṃ vindusaṃyuktaṃ na vāyurdvāranirgataḥ|yadālokopalabdhaṃ tu tat pariniṣpannalakṣaṇam

etāḥ prakṛtayaḥ sūkṣmāḥ śataṃ ṣaṣṭyuttaraṃ divā|rātrau cāpi pravartante vāyuvāhanahetunā

kṣaṇe lave muhūrtte ca nimeṣe mātrake tathā|kṣaṇa ityacchaṭāvasthā lavaḥ sarṣapavartanam

āśvāsastu muhūrttaṃ syānnimeṣo'kṣinimeṣaṇam|mātrā tu hastatālaṃ syāt kṣaṇādīnāṃ tu lakṣaṇam

saṃvittimātrakaṃ jñānamākāśavadalakṣaṇam|kintu tasya prabhedo'sti sandhyārātridivātmanā

ālokālokābhāsau ca tathālokopalabdhakam|cittaṃ trividhamityuktam ādhārastasya kathyate

vāyunā sūkṣmarūpeṇa jñānaṃ sammiśratāṃ gatam|niḥsṛtyendriyamārgemyo viṣayānavalambate

ābhāsena yadā yukto vāyurvāhanatāṃ gataḥ|tadā tatprakṛtīḥ sarvā astavyastāḥ pravartayet

yatra yatra sthito vāyustāṃ tāṃ prakṛtimudvahet|yāvat samīraṇotpādo nābhāso niścalo bhavet

ābhāsadvayahetuḥ syādātmabhāvavikalpanā|ubhayāṃśikameva syād yadālokopalabdhakam

sarvāsāmeva māyānāṃ strīmāyaiva viśiṣyate|jñānatrayaprabhedo'yaṃ sphuṭamatraiva lakṣyate

rāgaścaiva virāgaśca dvarorantariti trayam|dvīndriyasya samāpattyā vajrapadmasamāgamāt

jñānadvayasamāyogaḥ samāpattiḥ prakīrtītā|jñānadvayasamāpattyā yathoktakaraṇena tu

yajjñānaṃ prāpyate yatnāt tadālokopalabdhakam|yasya vajrābjasaṃyogaḥ saṃvṛtyā tu na vidyate

sidhyate yogasāmarthyāt sakṛdapyanubhūtavān|yathā prabhedaṃ vijñāya jñānavṛttiṃ svabhāvataḥ

lakṣayet satataṃ yogī tāmeva prakṛtiṃ punaḥ|payodharā yathā naike nānāsaṃsthānavarṇakāḥ

udbhūtā gaganābhogāllayaṃ gacchanti tatra vai|evaṃ prakṛtayaḥ sarvā ābhāsatrayahetukāḥ

nirviśya viṣayān kṛtsnān praviśanti prabhāsvaram|eṣāṃ svabhāvābijñānadajñānapaṭalāvṛtāḥ

kṛtvā śubhāśubhaṃ karma bhramanti gatipañcake|ānantaryādikaṃ kṛtvā narakeṣu vipacyate

śubhaṃ dānādikaṃ kṛtvā svargādiṣu mahīyate|anantajanmasāhasraṃ prāpya caivaṃ punaḥ punaḥ

pūrvakarmavipāko'yamiti śocati mohataḥ|prakṛtyābhāsayogena yena kliśyanti jantavaḥ

jñātvā tameva mucyante jñānino bhavapañjarāt|prajñāsvabhāva evāyaṃ candramaṇḍalakalpanā

cittameva svayaṃ paśyet svameva śaśibimbavat|atha candraṃ samālambya vajracinhaṃ prakalpayet

upāyasūcakaṃ hyetad vajrādyutpattiyoginām|candravajrādisaṃyogaścittacaitasasaṅgamaḥ

prajñopāyasamāyogājjāyate devatākṛtiḥ|caturmudrābhirāmudrya devatāgarvamudvahan

vicaret tu sadā mantrī utpattikramayogavān|yathoktaṃ śrīsamājādau tatra tatra suvistaram

yāvat syād bhāvanāyogastāvat syādādikarmikaḥ|pariniṣpannayogasya sūcana kriyate'dhunā

śūnyatrayaviśuddhiryā prabhāsvaramihocyate|sarvaśūnyapadaṃ tacca jñānatrayaviśuddhitaḥ

jñānaśuddhipadaṃ tattvaṃ sarvajñatvamanuttaram|nirvikāraṃ nirābhāsaṃ nirdvandvaṃ paramaṃ śivam

astīti na ca nāstīti na ca vākyagocaram|ataḥ prabhāsvarāccūddhājjñānatrayasamudbhavaḥ

dvātriṃśallakṣaṇadharo hyaśītivyañcanānvitaḥ|sarvākāravaropetaḥ sarvajño jāyate tataḥ

tathā coktaṃ mahāyānasūtre lalitavistare|abhisambodhikāmo'yaṃ śākyasiṃhastathāgataḥ

mahāśūnyena buddhatvaṃ prāpsyāmītyabhimānataḥ|nairañcanānadītīre nispādyāsphānakaṃ gataḥ

tilabimbīva sampūrṇāḥ khamadhyasthā jināstadā|ekasvareṇa taṃ prāhuracchaṭena jinaurasam

aviśuddhamidaṃ dhyānaṃ na caitadiṣṭakāvaham|prabhāsvaraṃ ātu ālambyamākāśatalavat param

prabhāsvarapade prāpte svecchārūpastu jāyase|savaiśvaryaṃ tathā prāpya vajrakāye pramodase

evaṃ śrutvā tu taṃ śbdaṃ visarjyāsphānakaṃ tataḥ|niśārdhasamaye tattvamālambyaiva jinaurasaḥ

ṛjukenaiva kāyena vācāya ṛjureva ca|sāśano nāśano naiva na maunī nāpyamaunavān

nonmīlitasunetrastu na ca mīlitalocanaḥ|svacchaṃ vyaktaṃ mahājñānaṃ sarvaśūnyaṃ mahādbhutam

atha paśyati tadvayaktaṃ gurupādaprasādataḥ|anāgatamatītaṃ ca vartamānaṃ bhavatrayam

tatkṣaṇānnikhilaṃ paśyet prabhāsvaraviśuddhadhṛk|jalacandramarīcyādimāyāguṇavibhūṣitaḥ

aruṇodgamakāle tu vajropamasamādhinā|niṣadya bodhimūle tu so'karonmārabhañcanam

samprāpya śākyanāthena tattvajñānamanuttaram|jagattrayahitārthāya tadeveha pradarśitam

tattvajñānamiti proktamabhisambodhidarśanam|pañcānantaryakarmā ca mandapuṇyo'pi yo naraḥ

guruprasādādāpnoti cintāmaṇirivāparam|yatheṣṭaṃ kurute caryāṃ saṃbuddho'yamanāgataḥ

na rāgo na virāgaśca madhyamā nopalabhyate|na śūnyaṃ nāpi cāśūnyaṃ madhyamā nopalabhyate

sarvabuddhasamāyoga idameva pradarśitam|trijñānād vyatiriktaṃ yat tattvaṃ sandhyāya bhāṣayā

abhāvetyādigāthābhiḥ paṭale bodhicittake|śrīsamāje'pi tat proktamabhisambodhilakṣaṇam

rāgādīnāṃ viśuddhiryā paramādye pradarśitā|sarvaśūnyaṃ samuddiśya sā'pi proktā tathāgataiḥ

nānāsūtreṣu tantreṣu yat tattvamupadarśitam|sarvaśūnyapadaṃ hyetannānyat tatrābhidhīyate

caturaśītisāhasre dharmaskandhe mahāmuneḥ|sārāt sārataraṃ proktam abhisambodhilakṣaṇam

jaṭī nagnaśca muṇḍī vā śikhiniḥsaṅgavṛttayaḥ|taistaiśca vividhairliṅgairabhisambodhikāminaḥ

teṣāṃ tattvavihīnānāṃ vratacaryādikaḥ kramaḥ|tattvajñānavihīnatvāt tena muktirna labhyate

ādikarmikayogena cāṣṭamīṃ bhūmimāpnuyāt|ālokatrayadarśī ca daśabhūmyāṃ pratiṣṭhitaḥ

samprāpya hyabhisambodhiṃ śuddhāvāsamupāgataḥ|buddhakṣetresvavaivartī sarvajña iha janmani

dharmodayābhisambodhiḥ krīḍārāgādivistaraiḥ|dharmadhātvābhisamabodhiryathālābhaviceṣṭitaiḥ

anutarābhisambodhirabhisambodhiyogataḥ|prapañcākārādicaryābhirabhyasyantīha yoginaḥ

āḥ kimabhyāsayogena ādiśuddhiḥ svabhāvikā|prakṛtyaiva hi sā siddhā tathatā na vikalpajā

ya evaṃ kalpayantīha jñānakramamapāsya vai|tatprabhedamajānānāḥ punaḥ śaikṣā bhavanti te

prakṛtyābhāsabhedajñā caturthaṃ tattvamāśritāḥ|tridhā nābhyasyate yastu na śīghramāpnuyāt phalam

yathāgnirdārugarbhastho nottiṣṭhenmathanād vinā|tathābhyāsād binā bodhirjāyate neha janmani

yaḥ śāṭhyabuddhiralaso gurunindakaścaprāptābhiṣeka iti garvitamānasaḥ syāt|sarvajñatā na sulabheti
vihīnacittodoṣān sa paśyati gurorna guṇān varākaḥ

sūśrūṣayā virahito laghu tattvamicchenneti praśstavacanaṃ calayet saroṣaḥ|dṛṣṭvā sabhāsu gurumasya
parāṅmukhastukuryāt praṇāmamatha tasya rahogatasya

evaṃ ca daurātmyagataṃ kuśiṣyamsvaputramapyaurasamāryagarhyam|vaiśyaṃ tathā pārthivamagrabodhiṃkuryāt
samīpe na hi jātu dhīraḥ

śubhaguṇasusameto jñānavān vīryayuktogurujanamatha bhaktyā vīkṣate buddhatulyam|adhigatajinadharmaḥ
śāsaneṣu prasannaḥsa iha bhavati pātraṃ tasya kuryāt prasādam

śrutabahutaratantro'uyāgameṣu pravīṇogurujanaparicaryāhānyalabdhopadeśaḥ|svahitamapi sa kartuṃ
na prabhuḥ śāstracañcu-rbhavati tadapi śāstraṃ kevalaṃ khedahetuḥ

atha bhavati sabhāgyaḥ prāptatattvopadeśojaḍamatirasamartho mīlane'rthasya yastu|parahitakṛtabuddhirdeśanāyāṃ
pravṛttovacanaguṇavihīnaḥ so'pyavajñāmupaiti

śrutabahutaratantro jñānavān ṣaṭpadajñaḥsmṛtimatidhṛtimedhāvīryasampatsametaḥ|gurucaṇasaparyāprāptatattvopadeśaḥprabhavati
sa hi vaktuṃ tantrarājopadeśam

śrutabahutaratantreṇāyavajriprasādātsphuṭaviracitavācā bodhimārgaṃ vibhajya|kuśalamupacittaṃ
yacchākyamitreṇa tenaprakaṭapaṭuvipākād bodhibhājo bhavantu||iti|94||anuttarasandhirityaparanāmā||sarvaśuddhiviśuddhikramaḥ
samāptaḥ||kṛtiriyaṃ śākyamitrapādānām| granthapramāṇamasya
śatamekam| dvitīyaḥ kramaḥ|
